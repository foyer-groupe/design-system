Roadmap

Our next steps leading by our Core Team. As a side project for the entire design team, we cannot make this roadmap more time sensitive.

  1. Image Library: to make a cross-domain library for Designer, Developer and Communication usages.
  2. Accessibility: Technical and Design Guideline on accessibility. (on going)
  3. Design Principles: re-work Foyer Design Principles and write them down.
  4. Foyer Public Website: Design System will also cover public websites.
  5. E-mailing & Postal Mailing: templating and communication standardization.
  6. Voice & Tone: how to communicate with end users (clients, agents, etc.)
  7. Vocabulary: standardization and simplification of the insurance terminology to avoid jargon and make the content more accessible.
  8. Document Base: to facilitate the access to documents for our agents and administration people.
  9. Press Kit: to make first communication more easy and standardized.
  10. Foyer Group: to make the Design System more global to the entire Foyer Group and not only the Insurance part of the company.
  11. Employee Kit: to onboard the newcomers more easily and efficiently.
